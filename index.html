About MPCZ

Built for the
Unforgiving World
of Nitro RC

Founded in Bergen, Norway in 1999, MPCZ was born from the belief that nitro RC engines deserve the same level of precision chemistry applied to full-scale racing powerplants. We refine and modify industry-grade engine protectants specifically for the extreme conditions inside a nitro RC engine — high RPM, elevated heat, and tight tolerances that leave no margin for error.

Norwegian Precision

Our formulations are developed against the exacting engineering standards of Norway's precision manufacturing sector. Every compound is selected for measurable performance — not marketing.

Not Off the Shelf

MPCZ products are not found in hobby stores or general retailers. Each formula is purpose-built for nitro RC use and distributed exclusively to serious competitors and enthusiasts.

Advanced Chemistry

Our formulas contain advanced ceramics, zinc-based anti-wear compounds, synthetic esters, PTFE friction reducers, and molybdenum disulfide technology — engineered in precise ratios for nitro RC fuel systems.

Racing Heritage

Twenty-five years of development driven by feedback from the track. MPCZ has been refined through thousands of hours of on-track use across multiple nitro RC racing disciplines worldwide.
