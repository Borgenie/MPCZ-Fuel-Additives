What It Does

Every Watt
To the Wheels

MPCZ Race is the most aggressive formula in the lineup. Formulated for engines being pushed to their limits in competition, it delivers the lowest achievable friction coefficient across all contact surfaces. The result is measurably more power reaching the drivetrain, tighter engine response, and sustained peak performance across the full race duration — not just the first lap.

Minimum Friction

Maximum-concentration PTFE and MoS₂ compounds reduce boundary and hydrodynamic friction to the lowest achievable values under race conditions.

Peak Power Output

By reducing parasitic friction losses, more combustion energy is converted to crankshaft rotation — delivering a measurable increase in usable power.

Thermal Stability

Race-grade synthetic esters remain fully stable at the extreme temperatures generated during continuous full-throttle competition running.

Sustained Performance

Protection does not degrade across race duration. MPCZ Race maintains its performance profile from the first lap to the last, with no fade.
