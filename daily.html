What It Does

Protection You
Run Every Tank

MPCZ Daily is designed for consistent use — added to every tank of nitro fuel. It maintains a protective film across all critical engine surfaces, reduces progressive wear, and keeps tolerances stable across temperature cycles. Think of it as the baseline that every other MPCZ formula builds on.

Wear Reduction

Zinc-based anti-wear compounds activate under load to prevent metal-to-metal contact at the sleeve, piston, and connecting rod bearings.

Film Stability

Synthetic ester carriers maintain a stable lubricant film even as fuel washes through the engine, resisting thermal breakdown at operating temperatures.

Ceramic Bonding

Advanced ceramics bond to cylinder and piston surfaces, providing a secondary protective layer that persists even when the fuel film is disrupted.

Extended Service

Consistent use measurably extends the interval between needle adjustments and engine rebuilds by slowing the rate of wear-related tolerance drift.
